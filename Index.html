About Me

I live in Littleton, Colorado with my husband and big fluffy cat. My spare time is spent playing music, exploring the outdoors, or working out at Awaken (Gymnastics Inspired Fitness). I have a passion for helping people find fun and sustainable ways to get in shape-- this pursuit is exactly what has set me free in the fitness world.


My Qualifications

ATC: Board certified and state licensed Athletic Trainer
CES: National Academy of Sports Medicine certified Corrective Exercise Specialist

My Background

I received my Bachelor's Degree in Athletic Training from Azusa Pacific University and then went to Regis University for my Masters and experience working as an Athletic Trainer with their Division II Women's Basketball team. In an effort to gain more experience with an adult/lay population, I have also worked in the Physical Therapy and Physician Sports Medicine setting. In addition to Freedom Fitness, I work as an Industrial Sports Medicine professional for some local distributorships.
